About Chia-Yuan

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISE) at Lehigh University under the supervision of Prof. Daniel P. Robinson. I received my M.S. from the Department of Industrial Engineering and Management in 2017, and my B.B.A. from the Department of Management Science at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University in 2015 in Hsinchu, Taiwan.

I currently work in the research area of fair machine learning, fair federated learning, and synthetic data generation. Before joining Lehigh University, I worked as a system design engineer at Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD) and AU Optronics Corp. (AUO) in Hsinchu, Taiwan.
